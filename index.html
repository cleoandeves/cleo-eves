WHY


We had been asked many times to build websites for friends and entrepreneurs in our network, but working full-time and having no time ourselves to build them we would try to find a trusted alternative, but failed to find one we could recommend with confidence. Website contracting has become overly complicated, all to take advantage of those who don't know what it takes (think: auto mechanics?). We wanted to recommend people who would really take care of the customer (and our friends and contacts), and the options seemed limited; you either have to have a very talented party who has a massive portfolio (think top $$$ websites in the 3K plus range), or use a "simple website building tool" like wix.com, squarespace, etc.


While we admire their work (squarespace is f'in awesome), there is still a large population who just wants a simple one pager that they can get up quickly and needs someone to help them walk through that.

That's how we came up with the one-form design that just requires the barebones text of your website, and ensured you would know everything you need up front in order to have your website done. We then turn them around in 3 business days upon receipt of a deposit. Simple? We think so.
